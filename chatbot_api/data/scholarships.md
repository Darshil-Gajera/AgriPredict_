# Agriculture Scholarships — Gujarat 2025-26

## MYSY Scholarship (Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana)
- **Eligible Courses**: B.Sc. Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry, Community Science; B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering, Food Technology, Biotechnology, Agri IT
- **Eligibility**: Gujarat domicile, 80%+ in 12th Science, family income ≤ ₹6 lakh/year
- **Benefit**: ₹50,000 tuition fee + hostel and book assistance
- **Apply at**: https://mysy.guj.nic.in

## National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
- **Eligible**: All UG courses including B.Sc. and B.Tech. Agriculture
- **Eligibility**: SC/ST/OBC/EWS/Minority; income limit applies per scheme
- **Benefit**: ₹10,000–₹30,000+ (tuition + maintenance)
- **Apply at**: https://scholarships.gov.in

## ICAR National Talent Scholarship
- **Eligible**: B.Sc. Agriculture, Horticulture, Forestry (ICAR seat holders only)
- **Eligibility**: Admission through ICAR AIEEA UG; non-domicile of that state
- **Benefit**: ₹2,000/month (₹24,000/year)

## Digital Gujarat Scholarship
- **Eligible**: All UG courses for SC/ST/OBC/SEBC/EWS students
- **Eligibility**: Gujarat domicile; income ₹2L–₹6L as per category
- **Benefit**: ₹5,000–₹20,000+ based on course and category
- **Apply at**: https://digitalgujarat.gov.in

## INSPIRE Scholarship (DST)
- **Eligible**: B.Sc. Agriculture, Biotechnology, Pure Science
- **Eligibility**: Top 1% in 12th Science stream
- **Benefit**: ₹80,000/year for up to 5 years
- **Apply at**: https://online-inspire.gov.in

## ONGC Scholarship for SC/ST/EWS
- **Eligible**: B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering, Food Technology; B.Sc. Agriculture
- **Eligibility**: SC/ST/EWS, 1st-year student, 60%+ in 12th, income ≤ ₹2 lakh
- **Benefit**: ₹48,000/year
- **Apply at**: https://ongcindia.com

## Kanya Kelavani Yojana (Girls Only)
- **Eligible**: All UG courses in Agriculture and Allied Sciences
- **Eligibility**: Gujarat domicile girl student
- **Benefit**: ₹5,000–₹25,000+
- **Apply at**: https://digitalgujarat.gov.in

## LIC Golden Jubilee Scholarship
- **Eligible**: All UG courses
- **Eligibility**: 60%+ in 12th, family income ≤ ₹2 lakh/year
- **Benefit**: ₹10,000–₹20,000/year
